A Look at Female Data in the Titanic

Conclusion

This visualization is based on the Titanic dataset available on Kaggle, which contains information about passengers on board the Titanic ship.
Our analysis focused on female data and we discovered interesting insights into the demographics and survival rates of women on the ship.
Overall, this project was completed by Mariam Alabboushi, Shaimaa Ali, and Saba Amjad, as part of a Decision Support Systems and Intelligent Systems course.
